Customer Intelligence & Revenue Optimization Platform

An Agentic AI platform that unified customer, support, product usage, and commercial data to uncover customer insights, revenue opportunities, and retention risks.

Business Challenge

  • Customer information was scattered across multiple enterprise systems.
  • Customer health and adoption signals were difficult to identify.
  • Cross-sell opportunities required manual investigation.
  • Business teams lacked a unified customer intelligence view.

Solution Overview

Built an Agentic AI platform using CrewAI, MCP, RAG, and Vector Search to provide customer intelligence, churn analysis, opportunity discovery, and AI-assisted decision support.
